About Us

Founder of Vedanta Academy, Ebran Alam is dedicated to providing quality IT, management, and vocational education. With a focus on practical skills and industry-aligned courses, he ensures students gain the knowledge and confidence needed to excel in the modern workforce.

Vedanta Academy is a registered Education Trust dedicated to accessible, high-quality technical education. We operate as a premier Computer Coaching Center, offering industry-aligned training crucial for today's digital economy.

Courses: DCA, DFA, GST, Tally Prime, ADCA, G-DCA, Advance Tally, Advance Excel, Typing Skills, etc.

Our Mission: To empower students from all backgrounds by bridging the digital skills gap and maximizing employability. As a non-profit trust, our focus is solely on student success, reinvesting resources into advanced learning infrastructure and student support.
